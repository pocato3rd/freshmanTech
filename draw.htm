How To Play

    Click and drag inside the canvas to draw. The drop down changes the canvas' background color and the color buttons change the color of your 'brush'. The size buttons change the size of your 'brush'. To clear the canvas click 'CLEAR'. This will set the canvas color to white and remove all points made. However, the color and size of the 'brush' will remain the same.

    When the size is :

  • red - your brush is 5px
  • green - your brush is 10px
  • blue - your brush is 20px
